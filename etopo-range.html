Earth Elevation Visualization

Subdivide the earth into 49,152 equal regions. How does the elevation in each region vary around the average elevation in that region?

This visualization shows the min, mean, and max elevation in each region, divided equally using HEALPix. You can also flip to view deep ocean trenches.

For more information on data sources, see earthtoposources.
