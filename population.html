This visualization shows the 2024 world population distribution using 3D truncated pyramids. Each region is a HEALPix cell (nside=32), with pyramid height proportional to the population in that region.

Technical Details: The data is loaded at nside=128 resolution and downsampled to nside=32 by summing populations. Each pyramid extends from radius 1 (Earth's surface) to an outer radius determined by the formula: r = (1 + 3p/(CA))^(1/3), where p is the population, A is the HEALPix pixel area, and C is a scaling constant chosen so the maximum radius is approximately 2.

Data Source:
WorldPop Global Demographic Data Project - Population Distribution (2024 estimates)
https://www.worldpop.org/
