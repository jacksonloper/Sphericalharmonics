Population Density Visualization

Visualizing global population density as "height" above the Earth's surface using HEALPix tessellation at nside=128 (196,608 regions).

Each HEALPix cell is represented as a truncated square pyramid with volume proportional to its population. This ensures accurate visual representation where the human eye naturally judges by volume rather than triangulated surface area.

Population data sources: LandScan / WorldPop datasets aggregated to HEALPix grid.
